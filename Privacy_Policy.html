Privacy Policy

Last updated: 26/03/2026

Your privacy is critically important to us. We have a few fundamental principles in regards to your privacy:

  • We don't ask you for personal information.
  • We don't store personal information on our servers.
  • Our services are supported by a number of third party service providers, including some located overseas in countries including the USA.

Below is our privacy policy which incorporates these goals. If you have questions about deleting or correcting your personal data please contact brent@wesley.email.

Website Visitors

SunoForge does not directly collect anonymous usage statistics. However, GitHub, who is the hosting provider for SunoForge, has the right to do so. Please read their privacy policy for further information.

GitHub General Privacy Policy

Cookies

SunoForge does not use cookies for the purpose of tracking sessions or usage.

Local Browser Storage

SunoForge, for the purpose of website functionality, may use your browser's Local Storage. At no point is this data saved to our servers. Protection of this information is the responsibility of the end user. This information may contain sensitive API keys for third party services.

3rd Party Data Storage Services

SunoForge, for the purpose of website functionality, may use 3rd party storage services (e.g. Google Drive) only with your consent and permission. At no point is this information stored on our servers and is provided directly to the 3rd party storage service via secured SSL encrypted transfers. Protection of the data stored by SunoForge is the responsibility of the end user.

Advertisements

SunoForge does not present any advertisements. No information is shared with 3rd party advertising companies.

Privacy Policy Changes

Although most changes are likely to be minor, SunoForge may change its Privacy Policy from time to time, and at SunoForge's sole discretion. SunoForge encourages visitors to frequently check this page for any changes to its Privacy Policy. Your continued use of this site after any change in this Privacy Policy will constitute your acceptance of such changes.
